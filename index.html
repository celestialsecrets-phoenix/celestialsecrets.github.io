Passive Income

Part of our revenue will be reinvested straight back to the project. Our vision is to be able to redistribute eth back to our community, so that essentially everyone is minting our token for free and be able to continuously earn more. To earn income, you can either participate in community events in our discord, or become a MUN membership in order to gain access to our main vault and earn passive income monthly.

So what exactly is our main vault? Each month, we are putting a portion of our revenue from multiple income sources into a vault that has been implemented in our smart contract and distribute that to everyone who has MUN membership(s).

So what revenue sources do we have in order to fund the main vault?
- Royalties from secondary sell
- Our very own crypto mining rig. The bigger our brand, the bigger our mining rig will be and the more passive income the community will earn.

In the future, you can expect more ways that our Celestials can earn income both actively and passively. Checkout our future plan in our roadmap.

DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations)

We are creating a web3 brand, not another web2 project on the blockchain. That means as a holder, you will get direct influence over how our project is heading toward the future.

The moment you become a Celestial, you are given the access to our DAO. We have implemented a voting system in our smart contract that allows our Celestials to vote on the important decisions of our project, this includes (but not limited to):
- How much fund from royalties and other sources of revenue will go into our main vault and how much will be reinvested into the brand, per month
- The charities that we are donating to

However, this doesn't mean we are going to take a step back and not do anything. Being decentralized doesn't mean there can't be a leader, so we are here to help drive our brand into a positive direction and execute the decisions that are being made by the community.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for Non-Fungible Token. To make it simple, an NFT is like a frontend and a backend of a digital product, the art represents the frontend and the utilities/benefits represent the backend.

Can I just screenshot it?

Hahaha, nope. All NFTs are verified through blockchain technology. Sure you'll get to save our awesome art as your screen saver, but there will be no utilities with it.

What is "Mint"?

Mint is the process of our NFT becomes a part of the blockchain. Basically you become the first owner of the token.

When is mint date and price?

- Price: 0.09 eth (could change depends on how Ether fluctuates. Price stays the same if Ether decreases but will drop if increases)
- Date: Some time in May 2022

How do I purchase?

All Celestials will be available to purchase on our website with Metamask extension. Additionally, you can mint via our smart contract using any crypto wallet. More details can be found in our Discord channel.

Can I resell or buy from a secondary market?

Yes, you can resell on secondary marketplace such as Opensea
